Analytics Inside - Advanced. Analytical. Intelligence.


Analytics Inside is an American company based in Westerville, Ohio. We were founded in 2005 and we specialize in advanced analytical solutions and services.

Analytics Inside produces the RelMiner family of relationship mining systems. These systems are based upon machine learning, big data, graph theory, data visualization, and natural language processing. Our products form an intergated suite of advanced analytical technologies based on current, scalable open source software.

We also deliver a wide range of training courses for these advanced technologies, and also consulting services for planning, management, and implementation, and integration of them.

In 2015, we published Learning Cascading which is available on Amazon.com.
